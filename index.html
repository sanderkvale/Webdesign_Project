ABOUT ME


Hi! My name is Ivy Chapel, I am a freelance photographer based in Perth at the beautiful west coast of Australia.


I recently graduated from my Bachelor of Arts in Photography and Illustration, and have decided to make a living of freelance photography.


Although I am relatively new to the industry, I have already managed to do a lot of projects for different clients.


My main specialties are fashion photography and editorial photography, as well as portrait photography. Although this is my specialties, I am still very open for projects in every industry.
